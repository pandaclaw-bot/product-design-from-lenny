# Brain Layer Add-on: Pricing

Use this file to reason about value capture and pricing structure.

## 1. Pricing definition

Pricing is the mapping from customer value to a chargeable, explainable, sustainable pricing unit and structure.

## 2. Core principles

### Principle A: Value before price
Clarify what business result the customer is buying.

### Principle B: Price unit should align with value unit
Prefer pricing structures that match how the customer experiences value.

### Principle C: Pricing is also positioning
A weak price signal can shrink perceived strategic value.

### Principle D: AI products require margin awareness
Inference, support, and implementation costs may distort gross margins.

## 3. Required questions

1. What result is being bought?
2. Who is the payer?
3. What pricing unit best fits value?
4. What pricing structure is easiest to explain and approve?
5. Where is the gross-margin risk?
6. How does pricing expand as the customer grows?

## 4. Anti-patterns

- copying competitor price points blindly
- using subscription as the whole answer
- hiding expensive AI value inside a low-price base package
- ignoring margin pressure from usage growth
